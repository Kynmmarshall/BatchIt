How it works

From your phone to your door
in 4 simple steps

No complicated logistics. No phone calls. Just open the app, join or start a batch, and let BatchIt handle the rest.

1

Open & Discover

BatchIt shows you live group purchases happening within 500 metres. See what your neighbours are buying right now.

2

Join or Start a Batch

Pick a product you need and request your share of the bulk quantity. Or start a new batch if it doesn't exist yet.

3

Auto-Order Fires

When enough people join and the bulk quantity is reached, BatchIt automatically places the group order. Zero manual effort.

4

Pick Up & Save

You'll get a notification when your order is ready. Collect your share from the local hub and enjoy bulk prices.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

BatchIt is built around one idea — making bulk buying effortless for everyone in your neighbourhood.

Live Map View

See active batches near you on a real-time map. Your exact location is never shared — only an approximate area.

Live Progress Tracking

Watch the batch fill up in real time. A clear progress bar shows exactly how many kilos or litres remain before your order fires.

Smart Notifications

Get alerted when a batch you've joined is nearly full, when your order is placed, and when it's ready for pickup.

Community Chat

Each batch has its own group chat. Coordinate with neighbours, ask questions, and build trust in your local community.

Dark & Light Mode

Comfortable at any time of day. BatchIt adapts to your preference — bright and clear in daylight, easy on the eyes at night.

French & English

Fully available in both French and English. Switch languages anytime from your profile with a single tap.

Installation guide

Installing in 3 minutes

Android makes it easy to install apps directly. Just follow these steps and you'll be buying with your community in no time.

1

Download the APK

Tap the "Download APK" button above. Your phone will download the BatchIt file — you'll see it in your notifications.

2

Allow unknown sources

When prompted, tap "Install anyway" or go to Settings → Security and enable "Install from unknown sources" for your browser.

3

Tap to install

Open the downloaded file from your notifications or file manager. Tap "Install" and wait a few seconds.

4

Open & sign up

Launch BatchIt, create your account, and let the app find batches near you. That's it — you're ready to save!

You may see a security warning — this is normal for apps installed outside the Google Play Store. BatchIt is safe and built by verified developers. The warning simply means it's a direct download, not a Play Store app (yet!).
